KISUM/MUSIK

Kisum (Jo Hye-ryung) born January 20, 1994 also known by her stage name Kisum is a south korean rapper who though relativley new to the korean rap game in 2013 has made a name for herself in the music scene by participating in the survial show "Unpretty Rapstar" in 2015, though she didn't win the compettion it only created more buzz for her in the long haul.

She is currently signed to MAPPS Entertainment
